Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a layered dish of pasta, a tomato based meat sauce and a mixture of cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 500g pound ground beef
  • 0.5 onions
  • 0.5 bell pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 can chopped tomatoes
  • 85g tomato puree
  • 2 tablespoons oregano
  • Handful freshly chopped parsley
  • 1 tablespoon italian seasoning
  • Pinch garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 250g lasagna noodles (dry)
  • 400g ricotta
  • 700g mozarella
  • 125g parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil pasta water in advance
  2. Heat olive oil in large pan and cook the beef until browned. Drain the fat away
  3. Fry the bell pepper, onion and garlic until soft then add the beef back into the pan
  4. Add tomatoes and all remaining ingredients except for the pasta and cheese. Bring the sauce to simmer and leave for 30 minutes
  5. Boil and drain the pasta noodles
  6. Assemble the lasagna in a baking dish by stacking sauce, pasta and cheese in that order in repeating layers
  7. Bake in the oven for 40 minutes
