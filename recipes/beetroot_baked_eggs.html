Beetroot Baked Eggs

Description

This dish is grated celeriac and beetroot with feta, yoghurt and spices, baked in the oven with an egg

Ingredients

  • 180g fresh beetroot
  • 180g celeriac
  • 3 tablespoons chopped dill
  • 180g natural yogurt
  • 1.5 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 180g feta
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180° C
  2. Mix together the grated beetroot, celeriac, dill yogurt, lemon juice and feta with a grind of black pepper
  3. Divide into 4 on a baking tray and make a small divet in the middle of all 4
  4. Place and egg in each of the 4 divets. Place in the oven for 8-10 minutes or until the eggs are fully baked
